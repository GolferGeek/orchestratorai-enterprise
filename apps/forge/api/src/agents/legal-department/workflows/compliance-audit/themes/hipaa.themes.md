# HIPAA Compliance Themes

## Theme: Use and Disclosure Standards
Rules: Privacy Rule
Questions:
- Are all uses and disclosures of PHI identified and documented?
- Is written authorization obtained for uses and disclosures not otherwise permitted?
- Is the minimum necessary standard applied to all uses and disclosures?
- Are policies and procedures in place for minimum necessary determinations?
- Are routine and recurring disclosures identified and limited to minimum necessary?
- Is there a process for reviewing non-routine disclosure requests?

## Theme: Individual Rights
Rules: Privacy Rule
Questions:
- Is there a process for handling access requests within 30 days?
- Can the organization provide PHI in the form and format requested if readily producible?
- Is there a process for handling amendment requests and documenting denials?
- Can an accounting of disclosures be provided for the prior six years?
- Is there a process for evaluating restriction requests?
- Can reasonable requests for confidential communications be accommodated?
- Are individuals informed of their rights under the Privacy Rule?

## Theme: Notice of Privacy Practices
Rules: Privacy Rule
Questions:
- Does the Notice of Privacy Practices include all required elements?
- Is the notice provided to individuals at the first point of service?
- Is the notice posted at physical premises where services are provided?
- Is the notice posted on the organization's website?
- Is a good faith effort made to obtain written acknowledgment of receipt?
- Is the notice updated when privacy practices change materially?

## Theme: Privacy Administrative Requirements
Rules: Privacy Rule
Questions:
- Is there a designated privacy official with defined responsibilities?
- Is there a designated contact person for complaints?
- Do all workforce members receive privacy training?
- Is training provided within a reasonable time of joining the workforce?
- Is there a sanctions policy for workforce members who violate privacy policies?
- Are all privacy policies and procedures documented?
- Is documentation retained for six years from date of creation or last effective date?

## Theme: Administrative Safeguards
Rules: Security Rule
Questions:
- Has a comprehensive risk analysis been conducted identifying threats and vulnerabilities to ePHI?
- Is there a risk management program to reduce risks to reasonable and appropriate levels?
- Is there a sanction policy for security violations by workforce members?
- Are information system activity reviews conducted regularly (audit logs, access reports)?
- Is there a designated security official responsible for security policies?
- Is workforce access to ePHI managed based on role and function?
- Do all workforce members receive security awareness training?
- Is there a contingency plan including data backup, disaster recovery, and emergency mode operations?
- Are security policies and procedures evaluated periodically?

## Theme: Physical Safeguards
Rules: Security Rule
Questions:
- Are facility access controls in place to limit physical access to ePHI systems?
- Is there a documented facility security plan?
- Are access control and validation procedures in place for facilities?
- Are maintenance records kept for physical security measures?
- Are there workstation use policies defining appropriate use?
- Are workstation security measures in place to prevent unauthorized physical access?
- Are device and media disposal procedures in place to ensure ePHI is removed?
- Are media re-use procedures in place to ensure ePHI is removed before re-use?
- Are accountability records maintained for hardware and media movement?

## Theme: Technical Safeguards
Rules: Security Rule
Questions:
- Does every system user have a unique user identification?
- Are emergency access procedures in place for accessing ePHI in emergencies?
- Is automatic logoff implemented after a period of inactivity?
- Is ePHI encrypted at rest using appropriate standards?
- Is ePHI encrypted in transit using appropriate standards?
- Are audit controls in place recording access and activity in ePHI systems?
- Is there a mechanism to authenticate ePHI integrity?
- Are person or entity authentication measures in place before granting access?
- Are transmission security measures implemented for ePHI sent over networks?

## Theme: Organizational Requirements
Rules: Security Rule
Questions:
- Are business associate agreements in place with all entities that handle ePHI?
- Do agreements include all required provisions?
- Do business associate agreements require implementation of appropriate safeguards?
- Do agreements require reporting of security incidents and breaches?
- Do agreements address subcontractor requirements?
- Is there a process for monitoring business associate compliance?

## Theme: Breach Notification
Rules: Breach Notification Rule
Questions:
- Are breach detection and investigation procedures documented?
- Is a risk assessment conducted to determine if breach notification is required?
- Can individual notification be provided within 60 days of discovery?
- Does notification include all required content (description, types of information, steps, entity actions, contact)?
- Is HHS notified for breaches affecting 500 or more individuals?
- Are prominent media outlets notified for breaches affecting 500 or more in a jurisdiction?
- Is an annual log submitted for breaches affecting fewer than 500 individuals?
- Do business associates notify covered entities within 60 days of discovery?
- Are all breach assessments and notifications documented?

## Theme: Unsecured PHI and Encryption
Rules: Breach Notification Rule
Questions:
- Is ePHI encrypted using NIST-approved methods?
- Are secure destruction procedures in place for PHI rendering it unreadable?
- Are encryption and destruction methods documented?
- Is there a process for assessing whether PHI involved in an incident was secured or unsecured?
- Are encryption standards reviewed and updated in line with current NIST guidance?

## Theme: Enforcement and Compliance
Rules: Enforcement Rule
Questions:
- Does the organization understand the penalty tiers and potential financial exposure?
- Is there a compliance program demonstrating reasonable diligence?
- Are identified violations corrected promptly within 30 days?
- Are compliance efforts and corrective actions documented?
- Is the organization prepared to cooperate with HHS investigations?

## Theme: Workforce Training and Awareness
Rules: Privacy Rule, Security Rule
Questions:
- Is privacy and security training provided to all workforce members upon hire?
- Is ongoing training provided when policies or procedures change?
- Is training documented with dates and attendance records?
- Does training cover the specific policies relevant to each workforce member's role?
- Are training effectiveness and comprehension assessed?
- Is there a process for addressing training deficiencies?

## Theme: Policies and Procedures
Rules: Privacy Rule, Security Rule
Questions:
- Are comprehensive written privacy and security policies maintained?
- Are policies reviewed and updated on a regular schedule?
- Are policies readily accessible to all workforce members?
- Do policies address all required Privacy Rule and Security Rule standards?
- Is there a formal change management process for policy updates?
- Are policy exceptions documented and approved through a formal process?

## Theme: Complaint Handling
Rules: Privacy Rule
Questions:
- Is there a documented process for receiving and investigating privacy complaints?
- Are complaints tracked and documented?
- Is there a designated contact for receiving complaints?
- Are complainants informed of the outcome of investigations?
- Does the organization prohibit retaliation against individuals who file complaints?
- Are complaint trends analyzed to identify systemic issues?
