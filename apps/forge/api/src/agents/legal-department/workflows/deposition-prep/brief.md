## Benefits

- **Turn matter materials into a usable deposition plan quickly.** The workflow organizes facts, witness background, prior statements, and supporting documents into a preparation outline an attorney can work from immediately.

- **Expose vulnerabilities before the witness is under oath.** Likely attack themes, inconsistent facts, and pressure points are surfaced early so prep time goes to the highest-risk issues.

- **Keep planning and rehearsal in one workspace.** The preparation outline, predicted cross-exam, and live simulation are connected so the team can move from analysis to practice without rebuilding context.

- **Support counsel instead of replacing counsel.** The workflow accelerates organization and issue spotting while leaving witness strategy, coaching, and final judgment to the attorney.

- **Keep work product private.** Witness themes, prior statements, and prep materials remain on your infrastructure.

## Features

- Preparation outline with themes, facts, vulnerabilities, and supporting materials
- Predicted cross-exam generation based on witness profile and case facts
- Shared workspace spanning outline, predicted cross-exam, and simulation
- Integration with matter context and prior workflow history
- Privilege-oriented handling for attorney work product
- Support for prior statements and witness background materials

## When to use it

- You are preparing a fact witness or corporate representative for deposition
- You need a fast first-pass prep outline before a live session
- You want to anticipate likely cross-examination themes
- You want a single workspace for preparation and practice

## How it works

1. Click **Prep a Deposition** and enter the witness details, case facts, and optional prior statements
2. Review the generated outline and highlighted risk areas
3. Open **Predicted Cross-Exam** to inspect likely questioning themes
4. Use **Simulation** to rehearse answers under pressure
5. Refine the prep strategy before the actual deposition
