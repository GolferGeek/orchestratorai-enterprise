## Benefits

- **See a range of trial outcomes instead of relying on a single instinctive forecast.** The simulator runs many structured trial variants so teams can evaluate likely outcomes, volatility, and downside scenarios before committing to strategy.

- **Identify which evidence and witnesses matter most.** Sensitivity analysis shows which factors actually shift win rates, damages exposure, or overall case posture.

- **Bring a more defensible settlement discussion to mediation or internal review.** The damages distribution gives teams a better basis for discussing exposure than a hand-waved number.

- **Stress-test strategy choices interactively.** Scenario filtering lets you explore what happens when key evidence is excluded or witnesses perform badly without rerunning the entire job.

- **Keep case facts and simulated outcomes inside your own infrastructure.** High-sensitivity matters stay local while the team explores strategic risk.

## Features

- Multi-run trial simulation across configurable case records
- Outcome distribution with verdict and damages summaries
- Sensitivity analysis for evidence and witness effects
- Client-side scenario builder for instant what-if exploration
- Dashboard views for progress, outcomes, sensitivity, and individual simulations
- Transcript access for each simulated run
- Configurable simulation count and execution model
- Persistent disclaimer across analytical outputs

## When to use it

- You are evaluating settlement range and trial exposure
- You need to prioritize evidentiary motions or witness prep resources
- You want quantified support for strategic discussions with clients or funders
- You want to pressure-test strategy against adverse trial conditions

## How it works

1. Click **Run New Simulation** and enter the case record
2. Choose the simulation count and review the projected cost
3. Acknowledge the limitations and launch the run
4. Monitor progress as simulations complete
5. Review the outcome distribution and damages summary
6. Use sensitivity and scenario tools to explore high-impact factors
7. Open individual simulation transcripts when you need deeper context
