KEY ASSUMPTIONS (BASE CASE):
- Top customer (TechCorp Industries) retention assumed at 100% through FY2028E. Current contract expires December 31, 2027 with a one-year automatic renewal unless cancelled 180 days prior.
- Diversification: "All other customers" bucket grows from $22.6M (FY2025) to $42.0M (FY2028E), reducing top-3 concentration from 67% to 58%.
- Gross margin improvement driven by pricing power and cost of revenue leverage.
- No major customer losses modeled. A loss of TechCorp Industries would reduce FY2026 revenue by $32M and push the Company into covenant breach territory on both Total Leverage and Fixed Charge Coverage ratios.

DOWNSIDE SENSITIVITY (NOT INCLUDED IN BASE CASE):
- Scenario: TechCorp Industries renegotiates at 20% lower volume in FY2027 → FY2027 revenue drops to $82.2M; Fixed Charge Coverage falls to 1.18x, covenant breach.
- Scenario: Loss of GlobalRetail Inc. in FY2026 → revenue impact $13.5M; would trigger material adverse change review under Facility 1.
