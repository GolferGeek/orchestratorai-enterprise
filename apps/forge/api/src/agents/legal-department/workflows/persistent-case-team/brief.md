## Benefits

- **Build a living matter record instead of re-learning the case from scratch each time.** As documents are added, the workflow continuously grows a matter-scoped knowledge base of people, organizations, dates, events, and document metadata.

- **Classify and summarize matter documents automatically.** Each uploaded file is typed, dated, summarized, and linked to key parties and terms without manual cataloging work.

- **Understand the cast of characters and chronology faster.** The workflow surfaces entities and timeline events across the matter so attorneys can orient themselves without reading everything first.

- **Make handoffs and prep work easier.** A persistent matter view reduces the friction of bringing in another attorney, preparing a witness, or revisiting a case after time away.

- **Keep case knowledge private.** Matter content, extracted facts, and timelines stay on your infrastructure.

## Features

- Parallel facts and document-processing agents for each upload
- Persistent matter-scoped checkpoint state
- Entity deduplication across the matter
- Document classification, dating, summarization, and metadata extraction
- Timeline event extraction and significance tracking
- Live processing visibility and matter statistics
- Matter dashboard views for case overview and documents

## When to use it

- You are opening a new matter and need to organize incoming documents quickly
- You expect rolling document inflow and want the matter record to improve over time
- You need a fast case handoff or re-orientation tool for another attorney
- You want witness, timeline, and document context available in one place

## How it works

1. Open **Case Team** from Legal Department and create a new matter
2. Enter the matter details and open its dashboard
3. Upload documents into the **Documents** tab
4. Let the workflow classify files and extract facts in parallel
5. Review the **Case Overview** for entities and timeline context
6. Continue adding documents as the matter evolves
