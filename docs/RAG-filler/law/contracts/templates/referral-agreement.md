## RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Company develops and operates the OrchestratorAI platform (orchestratorai.io), provides AI coding training and consulting services, and operates the Divinr.ai market prediction SaaS application;

WHEREAS, the Company desires to expand its market reach through qualified referral partnerships;

WHEREAS, Referral Partner has relationships and networks that may include prospective clients for the Company's products and services;

WHEREAS, the Parties desire to establish a referral relationship under which Referral Partner will introduce prospective clients to the Company in exchange for referral commissions;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants herein, the Parties agree as follows:

---

## SECTION 1. DEFINITIONS

1.1. **"Closed Deal"** means a Qualified Referral that results in the execution of a binding agreement between the Company and the Referred Prospect for one or more Company Products or Services, and for which the Company has received at least the first payment.

1.2. **"Company Products and Services"** means: (a) the OrchestratorAI platform and associated licenses; (b) AI consulting and training services; (c) Divinr.ai subscriptions; (d) hardware procurement services; and (e) any other products or services offered by the Company from time to time.

1.3. **"Confidential Information"** means all non-public information disclosed by either Party, including customer information, business strategies, pricing, technology, and referral program details.

1.4. **"Qualified Referral"** means a referral that meets the qualification criteria set forth in Section 3.3.

1.5. **"Referred Prospect"** means a prospective customer introduced to the Company by Referral Partner through the referral process described in Section 3.

1.6. **"Referral Commission"** means the compensation payable to Referral Partner for a Closed Deal, as specified in Section 4.

1.7. **"Referral Period"** means the one hundred eighty (180) day period following a valid referral submission during which a Closed Deal must occur for the Referral Commission to be payable.

---

## SECTION 2. APPOINTMENT AND SCOPE

2.1. **Non-Exclusive Appointment.** The Company hereby appoints Referral Partner as a non-exclusive referral partner. Referral Partner is not an exclusive sales representative, reseller, agent, or distributor of the Company.

2.2. **Scope.** Referral Partner may refer prospective clients for any Company Products and Services. Referral Partner shall focus its referral efforts on [describe target markets, if applicable: e.g., boutique legal firms, marketing agencies, financial advisory firms, mid-size companies with development teams] in [geographic area, if applicable].

2.3. **Non-Exclusive Relationship.** This Agreement does not prevent the Company from: (a) selling directly to any prospect, including those introduced by Referral Partner; (b) engaging other referral partners; or (c) accepting referrals from any source.

2.4. **No Authority to Bind.** Referral Partner has no authority to make representations, warranties, commitments, or promises on behalf of the Company. Referral Partner shall not negotiate pricing, contract terms, or service levels with Referred Prospects. All sales negotiations and contract execution shall be conducted by the Company.

## SECTION 3. REFERRAL PROCESS

3.1. **Referral Submission.** To submit a referral, Referral Partner shall:

(a) Submit a Referral Form (Exhibit A) to the Company via email to referrals@orchestratorai.io or through the Company's referral partner portal (if available);

(b) Include the following information: (i) Referred Prospect's company name and website; (ii) primary contact name, title, email, and phone number; (iii) brief description of the Referred Prospect's business and potential use case; (iv) estimated deal size (if known); and (v) any relevant context about the relationship and timing;

(c) Obtain verbal or written consent from the Referred Prospect before submitting the referral, confirming that the Referred Prospect is open to being contacted by the Company.

3.2. **Referral Acknowledgment.** The Company shall acknowledge receipt of a referral within five (5) business days and assign a tracking number. Within ten (10) business days, the Company shall notify Referral Partner whether the referral is accepted as a Qualified Referral.

3.3. **Qualification Criteria.** A referral qualifies if:

(a) The Referred Prospect is not already in the Company's active sales pipeline (a prospect is "active" if the Company has had substantive sales contact within the preceding ninety (90) days);

(b) The Referred Prospect is not an existing customer of the Company;

(c) The Referred Prospect is a legitimate business entity or decision-maker;

(d) The Referred Prospect has a genuine potential need for Company Products or Services;

(e) Referral Partner has a pre-existing relationship with the Referred Prospect; and

(f) The referral was submitted through the proper channels as described in Section 3.1.

3.4. **Disqualification.** A referral may be disqualified if: (a) it does not meet the criteria in Section 3.3; (b) the Referred Prospect is a competitor of the Company; (c) the same prospect has been referred by another referral partner who submitted earlier; or (d) the referral information is incomplete or inaccurate.

3.5. **Referral Tracking.** The Company shall maintain records of all referrals, their status, and outcomes. Referral Partner may request status updates on their active referrals, and the Company shall provide updates within five (5) business days of the request.

3.6. **Referral Expiration.** A Qualified Referral expires if a Closed Deal is not achieved within the Referral Period. Referral Partner may re-submit an expired referral, which will be treated as a new referral and subject to the qualification process.

## SECTION 4. REFERRAL COMMISSIONS

4.1. **Commission Schedule.** The Company shall pay Referral Partner the following commissions for Closed Deals:

**OrchestratorAI Platform Deployments:**
| Deal Component | Commission Rate | Basis |
|----------------|-----------------|-------|
| Initial deployment project fee | 10% | One-time project revenue |
| First-year annual license fee | 10% | Year 1 license revenue |
| Second-year annual license fee | 5% | Year 2 license revenue (if renewed) |
| Third-year annual license fee | 3% | Year 3 license revenue (if renewed) |

**Example:** For a $50,000 deployment project with a $20,000/yr Starter license:
- Year 1 commission: $5,000 (project) + $2,000 (license) = $7,000
- Year 2 commission: $1,000 (license renewal)
- Year 3 commission: $600 (license renewal)

**AI Training and Consulting Services:**
| Engagement Type | Commission Rate | Basis |
|-----------------|-----------------|-------|
| Training engagements | 10% | Total engagement revenue |
| Consulting engagements | 10% | Total engagement revenue |

**Divinr.ai Subscriptions:**
| Subscription Tier | Commission | Basis |
|-------------------|------------|-------|
| Essentials ($20/mo) | $50 | Per conversion (one-time) |
| Analyst ($75/mo) | $100 | Per conversion (one-time) |
| Professional ($200/mo) | $200 | Per conversion (one-time) |
| Enterprise ($500/mo) | $500 + 10% Year 1 | Flat fee + recurring |

**Hardware Procurement:**
| Deal Value | Commission Rate |
|------------|-----------------|
| Up to $50,000 | 5% |
| $50,001 - $200,000 | 3% |
| Over $200,000 | 2% |

4.2. **Commission Cap.** The maximum total commission payable for a single Closed Deal (across all years) shall not exceed $50,000 unless otherwise agreed in writing.

4.3. **Minimum Commission.** The minimum commission for any Closed Deal shall be $100.

4.4. **Payment Timing.** Commissions shall be paid as follows:

(a) **One-time commissions:** Within thirty (30) days after the Company receives payment from the Referred Prospect.

(b) **Recurring commissions (Year 2+):** Within thirty (30) days after the Company receives the applicable renewal payment.

(c) **Divinr.ai conversion commissions:** Within thirty (30) days after the Referred Prospect completes the first full month of paid subscription.

4.5. **Payment Method.** Commissions shall be paid via ACH direct deposit or check, at the Company's election. Referral Partner shall provide banking information or a valid mailing address for payments.

4.6. **Taxes.** Referral Partner is solely responsible for all taxes on commissions received. For U.S.-based Referral Partners, the Company shall issue IRS Form 1099-NEC for annual commissions exceeding $600. Referral Partner shall provide a completed W-9 prior to receiving any commission payment.

4.7. **Commission Adjustments.** The Company may adjust or claw back commissions if: (a) the Referred Prospect cancels within thirty (30) days of signing; (b) the Referred Prospect receives a significant price reduction after the deal closes that was not contemplated at closing; or (c) the Closed Deal is subsequently voided or rescinded.

4.8. **No Commission.** No commission is payable if: (a) the referral was not a Qualified Referral; (b) the Closed Deal occurred outside the Referral Period; (c) this Agreement was terminated before the Closed Deal; or (d) Referral Partner is in breach of this Agreement.

## SECTION 5. REFERRAL PARTNER OBLIGATIONS

5.1. **Professional Conduct.** Referral Partner shall conduct all referral activities in a professional manner that reflects positively on the Company's reputation and brand.

5.2. **Accurate Representations.** Referral Partner shall not make any false, misleading, or unauthorized representations about the Company, its Products and Services, pricing, capabilities, or guarantees.

5.3. **Approved Materials.** Referral Partner shall use only Company-approved marketing materials, descriptions, and messaging when discussing the Company with prospective clients. The Company shall provide Referral Partner with approved materials upon request.

5.4. **Compliance.** Referral Partner shall comply with all applicable laws and regulations, including anti-spam laws (CAN-SPAM, TCPA), anti-bribery laws (FCPA, if applicable), and data protection regulations.

5.5. **Reporting.** Referral Partner shall provide the Company with a quarterly summary of referral activities, including a pipeline of prospective referrals.

5.6. **Training.** Referral Partner agrees to participate in an initial orientation session and periodic product updates provided by the Company.

---

## SECTION 6. COMPANY OBLIGATIONS

6.1. **Referral Support.** The Company shall: (a) provide Referral Partner with approved marketing materials; (b) respond to referral submissions within five (5) business days; (c) provide regular status updates on active referrals; (d) handle all sales negotiations professionally; and (e) not intentionally circumvent Referral Partner to avoid paying commissions.

6.2. **Anti-Circumvention.** The Company acknowledges Referral Partner's role in introducing Referred Prospects and agrees not to take actions designed to avoid commission obligations, including redirecting Referred Prospects through other channels.

6.3. **Commission Transparency.** The Company shall provide Referral Partner with sufficient information to verify commission calculations, including deal values and applicable rates.

## SECTION 7. CONFIDENTIALITY

7.1. Each Party shall maintain the confidentiality of the other Party's Confidential Information, using at least the same degree of care as for its own confidential information, but not less than reasonable care.

7.2. Referral Partner shall not disclose the terms of this Agreement (including commission rates) to Referred Prospects or any third party.

7.3. Customer information shared by the Company with Referral Partner for referral tracking purposes shall be treated as Confidential Information and shall not be used for any purpose other than this Agreement.

7.4. Confidentiality obligations survive termination for three (3) years.

8.2. Referral Partner shall not modify the Company's trademarks or create derivative marketing materials without prior written approval.

8.3. All goodwill associated with the use of the Company's trademarks inures to the benefit of the Company.

10.1. NEITHER PARTY SHALL BE LIABLE FOR INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES.

10.2. THE COMPANY'S AGGREGATE LIABILITY SHALL NOT EXCEED THE TOTAL COMMISSIONS PAID TO REFERRAL PARTNER DURING THE TWELVE (12) MONTHS PRECEDING THE CLAIM.

## SECTION 11. TERM AND TERMINATION

11.1. **Term.** This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues for one (1) year, automatically renewing for successive one-year periods unless either Party provides thirty (30) days' written notice of non-renewal.

11.2. **Termination for Convenience.** Either Party may terminate upon thirty (30) days' written notice.

11.3. **Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate immediately if the other Party: (a) materially breaches and fails to cure within fifteen (15) days of notice; (b) engages in conduct that damages the terminating Party's reputation; or (c) violates Section 5.2 or 5.4.

11.4. **Effect of Termination.** Upon termination: (a) Referral Partner shall cease all referral activities; (b) commissions for Qualified Referrals submitted before termination that result in Closed Deals within the Referral Period remain payable; (c) recurring commissions (Year 2+) for deals closed before termination remain payable for the remaining commission period; (d) each Party shall return the other's Confidential Information; and (e) Referral Partner shall cease using the Company's trademarks within fifteen (15) days.

11.5. **Survival.** Sections 4 (for earned commissions), 7, 9.3, 10, and 12 survive termination.
