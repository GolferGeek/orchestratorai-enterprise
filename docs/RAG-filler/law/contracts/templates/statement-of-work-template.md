## SECTION 4. ACCEPTANCE CRITERIA AND PROCESS

4.1. **Deliverable Acceptance.** For each Deliverable, the following acceptance process applies:

(a) **Submission.** Provider shall submit the Deliverable to Client with a written notice that the Deliverable is ready for acceptance review.

(b) **Review Period.** Client shall have five (5) business days (the "Review Period") to review the Deliverable and determine whether it meets the applicable acceptance criteria.

(c) **Acceptance or Rejection.** Within the Review Period, Client shall either: (i) accept the Deliverable by providing written acceptance; or (ii) reject the Deliverable by providing a written rejection notice specifying in reasonable detail the deficiencies.

(d) **Deemed Acceptance.** If Client fails to provide written acceptance or rejection within the Review Period, the Deliverable shall be deemed accepted.

(e) **Cure Period.** Upon rejection, Provider shall have ten (10) business days (the "Cure Period") to correct the deficiencies and re-submit the Deliverable. Client shall have an additional five (5) business days to review the corrected Deliverable.

(f) **Escalation.** If a Deliverable fails acceptance after two (2) cure cycles, the matter shall be escalated to the project sponsors identified in Section 5 for resolution.

4.2. **Milestone Acceptance.** Milestone acceptance requires written acceptance of all Deliverables within that Phase.

4.3. **Final Acceptance.** Final project acceptance occurs when all milestones have been accepted and the post-go-live support period has concluded without unresolved critical issues.

## SECTION 8. CHANGE ORDER PROCESS

8.1. **Change Requests.** Either Party may request a change to the scope, timeline, or fees of this SOW by submitting a written Change Request to the other Party's Project Manager using the Change Request Form (Exhibit C).

8.2. **Impact Assessment.** Upon receiving a Change Request, Provider shall provide a written impact assessment within five (5) business days, including: (a) description of the change; (b) impact on scope, timeline, and fees; (c) alternative approaches (if applicable); and (d) recommendation.

8.3. **Approval.** No change shall be effective until both Parties have signed a Change Order documenting the agreed-upon change. Client's Project Sponsor must approve all Change Orders.

8.4. **Work Stoppage.** Provider shall not stop work on unaffected portions of the SOW while a Change Request is pending, unless the change fundamentally alters the project direction and continued work would be wasted.

8.5. **Cumulative Changes.** If cumulative Change Orders increase the total project fee by more than twenty percent (20%) or extend the timeline by more than thirty (30) calendar days, the Parties shall conduct a comprehensive project review and may negotiate an amended SOW.

## SECTION 9. TERM AND TERMINATION

9.1. **Term.** This SOW commences on the Effective Date and continues until Final Acceptance or termination, whichever occurs first.

9.2. **Termination.** This SOW may be terminated in accordance with the termination provisions of the MSA. In addition, either Party may terminate this SOW upon thirty (30) days' written notice if progress is materially stalled for reasons attributable to the other Party.

9.3. **Effect of Termination.** Upon termination: (a) Client shall pay for all Services performed and approved Deliverables delivered through the effective date of termination; (b) Provider shall deliver all completed and in-progress Deliverables; (c) for fixed-fee engagements, payment shall be prorated based on the percentage of each Phase completed; and (d) the MSA shall continue in effect.
