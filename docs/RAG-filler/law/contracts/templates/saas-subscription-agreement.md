2.1. **Service Description.** Divinr.ai is a market prediction and analysis platform that utilizes artificial intelligence and machine learning models to: (a) analyze market data, news, and financial indicators; (b) generate predictive scores and confidence assessments for market instruments; (c) provide trend analysis and pattern recognition; (d) deliver automated alerts based on Subscriber-defined criteria; and (e) present data visualizations and analytical dashboards.

2.2. **Service Access.** Subject to the terms of this Agreement and payment of the Subscription Fee, Provider grants Subscriber a non-exclusive, non-transferable, revocable right to access and use the Service during the Subscription Period through a standard web browser or supported client application.

2.3. **Subscription Tiers.** The Service is offered under the following subscription tiers:

**Essentials Tier - $20.00 per month:**
- Access to daily market prediction summaries
- Up to 5 watchlist instruments
- Basic trend analysis dashboard
- Email alerts (up to 10 per week)
- 7-day prediction history
- Community support

**Analyst Tier - $75.00 per month:**
- All Essentials features, plus:
- Real-time prediction updates
- Up to 25 watchlist instruments
- Advanced analytics dashboard with custom timeframes
- Email and SMS alerts (unlimited)
- 90-day prediction history
- Sector and industry analysis
- Custom query builder
- Email support (24-hour response)

**Professional Tier - $200.00 per month:**
- All Analyst features, plus:
- Up to 100 watchlist instruments
- API access (10,000 calls/month)
- 1-year prediction history
- Portfolio-level risk analysis
- Multi-factor prediction models
- Custom alert logic
- Exportable reports (CSV, PDF)
- Priority email support (8-hour response)

**Enterprise Tier - $500.00 per month:**
- All Professional features, plus:
- Unlimited watchlist instruments
- API access (100,000 calls/month)
- Full prediction history
- Custom model training on Subscriber data
- Dedicated prediction models
- White-label dashboard option
- Webhook integrations
- Dedicated support representative
- Quarterly strategy review call

2.4. **Annual Billing Discount.** Subscribers who elect annual billing shall receive a twenty percent (20%) discount on the applicable monthly rate. Annual subscriptions are billed as a single payment at the start of each annual Subscription Period.
