## SECTION 1. DEFINITIONS

1.1. **"AI Training Services"** means instruction, mentoring, hands-on workshops, code review sessions, curriculum development, and related educational services in the field of artificial intelligence, machine learning, and AI-assisted software development.

1.2. **"Client Materials"** means all code, data, documentation, specifications, and other materials provided by Client to Consultant in connection with the Services.

1.3. **"Confidential Information"** means all non-public information disclosed by either Party to the other in connection with this Agreement, including but not limited to business plans, financial information, technical data, trade secrets, customer information, and proprietary methodologies.

1.4. **"Consulting Services"** means technical advisory, architecture review, code audit, technology evaluation, and related professional consulting services.

1.5. **"Deliverables"** means all work product, training materials, reports, assessments, and other materials created by Consultant specifically for Client in the performance of the Services.

1.6. **"OrchestratorAI Platform"** means Consultant's proprietary AI orchestration platform available at orchestratorai.io, including all associated software, tools, and documentation.

1.7. **"Services"** means the AI Training Services, Consulting Services, and any other professional services described in one or more Engagement Orders executed pursuant to this Agreement.

1.8. **"Engagement Order"** means a written document executed by both Parties describing the specific Services, timeline, fees, and deliverables for a particular engagement, substantially in the form attached hereto as Exhibit A.

1.9. **"Training Materials"** means curricula, slide decks, code examples, exercises, lab environments, reference guides, and all other instructional materials used or created in connection with AI Training Services.

## SECTION 2. SCOPE OF SERVICES

2.1. **Service Categories.** Consultant offers the following categories of Services, any of which may be engaged through an Engagement Order:

(a) **AI Coding Training.** Hands-on instruction in AI/ML development, including but not limited to: Python for AI, prompt engineering, LLM integration, agent development, RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) architectures, LangChain/LangGraph frameworks, NestJS API development, and Vue.js frontend development for AI applications.

(b) **Architecture Consulting.** Technical advisory services for designing and implementing AI systems, including multi-agent architectures, provider plane abstractions, local deployment topologies, and hybrid cloud/on-premises AI infrastructure.

(c) **Code Audit and Review.** Systematic review of Client's existing codebase for AI readiness, security posture, architectural quality, and optimization opportunities.

(d) **Platform Evaluation.** Assessment and recommendation services for AI platform selection, with demonstrations of the OrchestratorAI platform capabilities where relevant to Client's needs.

(e) **Custom Curriculum Development.** Creation of tailored training programs aligned with Client's specific technology stack, skill gaps, and business objectives.

2.2. **Engagement Orders.** Each engagement shall be documented in a separate Engagement Order that specifies: (a) the type and scope of Services; (b) the timeline and schedule; (c) the applicable fee structure; (d) specific Deliverables; and (e) any special terms or conditions.

2.3. **Professional Standards.** Consultant shall perform all Services in a professional manner consistent with industry standards for technology consulting and training. Consultant shall assign qualified personnel with relevant expertise in the subject matter of each Engagement Order.

2.4. **Client Cooperation.** Client shall provide Consultant with: (a) reasonable access to Client's facilities, systems, and personnel as necessary for the performance of Services; (b) all Client Materials reasonably requested by Consultant; (c) timely decisions and approvals; and (d) a suitable workspace and equipment for on-site engagements, including presentation equipment, internet connectivity, and development environments.

## SECTION 3. COMPENSATION AND PAYMENT

3.1. **Hourly Rate Structure.** Unless otherwise specified in an Engagement Order, Services shall be billed at the following hourly rates:

| Service Category | Standard Rate | Volume Rate (40+ hrs/mo) |
|------------------|---------------|--------------------------|
| AI Coding Training (Individual) | $100.00/hr | $90.00/hr |
| AI Coding Training (Group, up to 8) | $100.00/hr | $90.00/hr |
| AI Coding Training (Group, 9-20) | $150.00/hr | $135.00/hr |
| Architecture Consulting | $100.00/hr | $90.00/hr |
| Code Audit and Review | $80.00/hr | $72.00/hr |
| Custom Curriculum Development | $90.00/hr | $80.00/hr |

3.2. **Minimum Engagement.** Each on-site training or consulting session shall have a minimum engagement of two (2) hours. Remote sessions shall have a minimum engagement of one (1) hour.

3.3. **Rate Adjustments.** Consultant reserves the right to adjust rates annually, effective on each anniversary of the Effective Date, upon thirty (30) days' prior written notice to Client. Rate increases shall not exceed five percent (5%) per year. Rate adjustments shall not apply to active Engagement Orders.

3.4. **Fixed-Fee Engagements.** For defined-scope projects, the Parties may agree to a fixed fee in the applicable Engagement Order. Fixed-fee engagements shall include a detailed scope definition and a change order process for scope modifications.

3.5. **Invoicing.** Consultant shall submit invoices to Client on a bi-weekly basis or upon completion of each Engagement Order, whichever occurs first. Each invoice shall include: (a) the Engagement Order reference; (b) a description of Services performed; (c) the dates and hours worked; (d) the applicable rate; and (e) any approved expenses.

3.6. **Payment Terms.** Client shall pay all undisputed invoices within thirty (30) calendar days of receipt. Late payments shall accrue interest at the rate of one and one-half percent (1.5%) per month, or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is less.

3.7. **Travel and Expenses.** For on-site engagements at Client's facilities:

(a) **Local (within 50 miles of Minneapolis/St. Paul).** No additional travel charges.

(b) **Regional (50-250 miles).** Client shall reimburse mileage at the current IRS standard mileage rate, plus reasonable meal expenses not to exceed $75 per day.

(c) **National/International.** Client shall reimburse reasonable travel expenses, including: (i) round-trip airfare (economy class for flights under 4 hours, business class for flights over 4 hours); (ii) hotel accommodations not to exceed $250 per night; (iii) ground transportation; and (iv) meals not to exceed $100 per day.

(d) **Pre-Approval.** All travel expenses exceeding $500 in aggregate for a single trip must be pre-approved by Client in writing.

3.8. **Cancellation.** Client may cancel or reschedule a confirmed session with the following notice requirements:

(a) More than 48 hours' notice: no charge.
(b) 24-48 hours' notice: 50% of the scheduled session fee.
(c) Less than 24 hours' notice or no-show: 100% of the scheduled session fee.

## SECTION 8. TERM AND TERMINATION

8.1. **Term.** This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for an initial period of one (1) year (the "Initial Term"). Thereafter, this Agreement shall automatically renew for successive one-year periods unless either Party provides written notice of non-renewal at least sixty (60) days prior to expiration of the then-current term.

8.2. **Termination for Convenience.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement upon thirty (30) days' prior written notice. Termination of this Agreement shall not affect any active Engagement Orders unless specifically stated in the termination notice.

8.3. **Termination for Cause.** Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party: (a) materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure within fifteen (15) days of written notice; or (b) becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy.

8.4. **Effect of Termination.** Upon termination: (a) Client shall pay for all Services performed through the effective date of termination; (b) each Party shall return the other's Confidential Information; and (c) all Engagement Orders shall terminate unless otherwise agreed.

8.5. **Survival.** Sections 4, 5, 6.3, 7, and 9 through 11 shall survive termination.
