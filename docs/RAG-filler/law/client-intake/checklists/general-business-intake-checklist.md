# General Business Client Intake Checklist

**Document Type:** Universal Client Intake Checklist — All OrchestratorAI Clients
**Prepared By:** OrchestratorAI Practice Management Consulting
**Version:** 1.0
**Last Updated:** April 2026
**Applicable To:** Any business evaluating or onboarding onto the OrchestratorAI platform, or any new client of an OrchestratorAI-powered professional services firm

---

## Purpose

This is the universal intake checklist for any new OrchestratorAI client. It captures the baseline information required regardless of industry vertical, company size, or engagement type. Industry-specific checklists (technology, legal, financial services, marketing) supplement this document with vertical-specific requirements.

Use this checklist when:
- The prospective client does not fit neatly into an industry-specific checklist
- You need a quick-start intake before applying a specialized checklist
- The engagement is limited in scope and does not warrant a full industry-specific intake
- The client operates across multiple industries

## Section 10: Post-Intake Workflow

After completing this checklist:

1. **Within 24 hours:** Enter all information into the CRM and engagement management system
2. **Within 48 hours:** Complete conflict check and document results
3. **Within 48 hours:** Distribute intake summary to assigned team members
4. **Within 3 business days:** Issue engagement letter, MSA, or proposal
5. **Within 5 business days of execution:** Schedule deployment kickoff (for platform clients) or matter kickoff (for legal/consulting clients)
6. **Within 10 business days of execution:** Complete initial platform deployment or begin substantive work
