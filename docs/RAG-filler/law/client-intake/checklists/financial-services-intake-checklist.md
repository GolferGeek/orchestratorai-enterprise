## Purpose

Financial services clients operate under the most stringent regulatory regimes in the US economy. This checklist addresses the unique requirements of onboarding financial firms, including SEC and FINRA registration status, fiduciary obligations, data sensitivity classifications, existing prediction and analysis tools, compliance frameworks, and risk tolerance for AI-assisted decision-making.

This document serves both OrchestratorAI's direct sales team and boutique legal or consulting firms using the OrchestratorAI knowledge base to onboard their own financial services clients.

## Section 5: AI Risk Tolerance Assessment

### 5.1 AI Decision-Making Framework

This section evaluates the firm's risk tolerance for AI-assisted and AI-automated decision-making. OrchestratorAI provides capabilities ranging from research assistance to automated trading signals. The firm's risk tolerance determines the appropriate deployment configuration.

- [ ] **Level 1 — AI as Research Assistant (Low Risk):**
  - [ ] AI-assisted document review and summarization
  - [ ] AI-assisted regulatory research
  - [ ] AI-assisted client communication drafting
  - [ ] All outputs reviewed by human before action
  - Risk tolerance: Conservative

- [ ] **Level 2 — AI as Analytical Tool (Moderate Risk):**
  - [ ] AI-generated market analysis and reports
  - [ ] AI-assisted portfolio screening and filtering
  - [ ] AI-generated risk assessments
  - [ ] Outputs inform human decisions but do not directly execute
  - Risk tolerance: Moderate

- [ ] **Level 3 — AI as Decision Support (Elevated Risk):**
  - [ ] AI-generated trade recommendations with confidence scoring
  - [ ] AI-driven portfolio rebalancing suggestions
  - [ ] AI-assisted compliance monitoring with automated alerts
  - [ ] Divinr.ai market prediction integration for analysis
  - [ ] Human approval required before execution
  - Risk tolerance: Moderate-High

- [ ] **Level 4 — AI-Augmented Automation (High Risk):**
  - [ ] AI-generated trade signals with automated execution (human override available)
  - [ ] AI-driven client communications (sent after automated review)
  - [ ] Automated compliance reporting
  - [ ] Requires robust governance framework and audit trail
  - Risk tolerance: High

- [ ] Selected risk tolerance level: _______________
- [ ] Regulatory implications of selected level documented
- [ ] Client disclosure requirements for selected level identified
- [ ] Governance and oversight procedures for selected level defined

## Notes

- Financial services intakes are the most compliance-intensive of any vertical. The CCO must be involved from the beginning, not brought in after decisions are made.
- MNPI handling is a critical consideration. If the firm trades on proprietary information, OrchestratorAI must be deployed in sovereign mode with no cloud data transmission.
- AI risk tolerance discussions are essential and should not be rushed. The regulatory implications of AI-assisted trading decisions are evolving rapidly. Document the firm's chosen level and the regulatory analysis supporting it.
- Divinr.ai integration requires additional due diligence on the firm's side regarding whether AI-generated market predictions constitute investment advice under their regulatory framework.
- Books and records requirements (SEC Rule 204-2, FINRA Rule 4511) apply to AI-generated analysis and recommendations. Ensure the deployment includes appropriate audit trails.
