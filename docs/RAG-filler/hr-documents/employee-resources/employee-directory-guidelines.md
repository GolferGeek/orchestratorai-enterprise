## Maintaining Your Profile

### Updating Your Information

**To Update:**
1. Log into Workday
2. Go to Personal Information
3. Click "Edit"
4. Update relevant fields
5. Submit changes

**What You Can Update:**
- Preferred name (how you want to be called)
- Pronouns
- Profile photo
- Work phone/extension
- Bio and skills
- Location (if you move)

**What Requires HR Approval:**
- Legal name change
- Department transfer
- Job title change
- Manager change

### Profile Photo Guidelines

**Recommended:**
- Professional headshot
- Clear, well-lit image
- Recent photo (within 2 years)
- Face clearly visible
- Plain or neutral background

**Avoid:**
- Group photos
- Inappropriate images
- Low quality or blurry photos
- Photos with sunglasses or hats
- Heavily filtered images

**How to Upload:**
1. Go to Workday
2. Click on your profile
3. Select "Change Photo"
4. Upload image (JPEG, PNG; max 5MB)
5. Crop and save

### Pronouns

You may add your pronouns:
- She/Her
- He/Him
- They/Them
- Other options available

Adding pronouns is voluntary and helps colleagues address you correctly.

## Using the Directory

### Finding Colleagues

**Search Tips:**
- Search by first or last name
- Search by department or team
- Use partial names if unsure of spelling
- Search by job title to find experts

### Understanding the Org Chart

The directory includes org chart view:
- See reporting relationships
- View team structures
- Navigate up and down the hierarchy
- Click names to see profiles

### Contacting Colleagues

From a directory profile, you can:
- Send email (click email address)
- Call (click phone number)
- Start Teams chat (click Teams icon)
- Send Slack message (click Slack icon)
- Schedule meeting (view calendar availability)

## Privacy and Security

### What Employees Should Know

- Directory is for internal business use only
- Do not share directory data externally
- Do not use for commercial or personal purposes
- Report any misuse to HR or IT

### Restricting Your Information

In limited circumstances, you may request to restrict certain information:
- Safety concerns (domestic violence, stalking)
- Other legitimate reasons

**To Request:**
Contact HR confidentially at hr@acmecorp.com

### Data Protection

- Directory access requires Acme login
- Data protected by security controls
- Access logged for audit purposes
- External access not permitted

## Common Questions

**Q: How do I change my name in the directory?**
A: Preferred name can be changed in Workday. Legal name changes require HR documentation.

**Q: Why isn't my information showing correctly?**
A: Allow 24-48 hours for updates to propagate. If still incorrect, contact HR.

**Q: Can I hide my phone number?**
A: Standard work contact info is visible. For safety concerns, contact HR.

**Q: How do I add skills to my profile?**
A: In Workday, go to Career > Skills and Expertise.

**Q: Can contractors access the directory?**
A: Yes, contractors have directory access for business purposes.

**Q: How do I report someone misusing directory data?**
A: Contact HR or IT Security immediately.
