## CloudRiver Analytics, Inc. Master Services Agreement

Effective date: March 1, 2026  
Vendor: CloudRiver Analytics, Inc., a Delaware corporation  
Customer: Northstar Retail Group, LLC, an Illinois limited liability company  
Initial term: 24 months  
Governing law: Delaware

## 1. Services

CloudRiver will provide access to its hosted analytics platform, implementation support, and standard customer support. CloudRiver may modify the platform, documentation, security procedures, and service descriptions at any time if CloudRiver determines the change is commercially reasonable.

## 2. Customer data

Customer grants CloudRiver a worldwide, royalty-free license to host, copy, process, analyze, transform, benchmark, and otherwise use Customer Data to provide the services, improve CloudRiver products, develop new features, create aggregated insights, and support CloudRiver’s business operations. CloudRiver may retain aggregated or de-identified data after termination.

Customer Data may include sales data, customer contact data, loyalty program data, purchase history, product returns, support notes, and limited payment-related metadata. Customer represents that it has obtained all rights, consents, and notices required for CloudRiver to process Customer Data.

## 3. Security

CloudRiver will maintain commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards. CloudRiver currently targets SOC 2 Type II completion during the 2026 calendar year but does not guarantee certification by any specific date. CloudRiver will notify Customer of confirmed unauthorized access to Customer Data without undue delay after CloudRiver completes its internal investigation.

Customer may request CloudRiver’s security questionnaire once per contract year. CloudRiver may decline requests that reveal confidential architecture information.

## 4. Subprocessors

CloudRiver may use affiliates, contractors, hosting providers, model providers, analytics vendors, and other subprocessors to provide and improve the services. CloudRiver will maintain a public subprocessor page and may update it from time to time. Customer’s continued use of the services after an update constitutes acceptance of new subprocessors.

## 5. Fees and renewal

Fees are $38,000 per year, invoiced annually in advance. The agreement renews automatically for successive one-year periods unless either party gives notice of non-renewal at least 90 days before the end of the then-current term. Fees for renewal terms may increase by up to 12% per year.

## 6. Intellectual property

CloudRiver owns the platform, documentation, usage data, performance data, configurations, workflows, reports, templates, model prompts, model outputs generated by CloudRiver systems, and all improvements or derivatives. Customer owns Customer Data. If Customer suggests improvements or provides feedback, CloudRiver may use that feedback without restriction or obligation.

## 7. Confidentiality

Each party will protect the other party’s confidential information using reasonable care. Confidential information excludes information that becomes public, is independently developed, or is received from a third party without restriction. Confidentiality obligations survive for three years after termination, except trade secrets survive only as long as they remain trade secrets under applicable law.

## 8. Indemnity

Customer will defend and indemnify CloudRiver against claims arising from Customer Data, Customer’s use of the services, alleged violation of privacy law, and Customer’s breach of this agreement. CloudRiver will defend Customer against third-party claims that the unmodified platform infringes a United States patent or copyright. CloudRiver may terminate the affected service if it determines an infringement claim is likely.

## 9. Limitation of liability

Except for Customer’s payment obligations and Customer’s indemnity obligations, each party’s total liability will not exceed fees paid in the twelve months before the event giving rise to liability. Neither party will be liable for indirect, consequential, special, punitive, or lost-profit damages, even if advised of the possibility of such damages.

## 10. Termination

Either party may terminate for material breach if the breach remains uncured 30 days after written notice. CloudRiver may suspend access immediately for non-payment, suspected security risk, legal compliance concerns, excessive usage, or conduct that may harm CloudRiver systems or reputation.

## 11. Data export and deletion

For 30 days after termination, Customer may request export of Customer Data in CloudRiver’s standard export format. After that period, CloudRiver may delete Customer Data from active systems on its normal deletion cycle. Backup deletion may take up to 180 days.

## 12. Assignment

Customer may not assign this agreement without CloudRiver’s prior written consent. CloudRiver may assign this agreement in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, corporate reorganization, sale of assets, or transfer to an affiliate.
