# Document Onboarding Workflow Dossier

Document Onboarding is the front door for the Legal Department showcase. It turns an unknown incoming legal document or packet into a structured first-pass legal assessment that a professional can review, correct, and use to decide the next step.

## Dossier contents

- `benefits-brief.md` — user-facing value story for professionals evaluating the workflow
- `demo-guide.md` — video outline, talk track, and screen-by-screen guidance
- `test-plan.md` — repeatable test scenarios and expected workflow behavior
- `test-documents/` — synthetic legal documents designed to exercise specialist routing
- `document-onboarding-briefing.pptx` — short presentation deck for walkthroughs and stakeholder briefings

## Recommended demo path

1. Start with `scenario-01-saas-msa-high-risk.md` to show the workflow catching cross-domain risk in a familiar commercial contract.
2. Upload all three scenario documents together to show packet handling and specialist routing.
3. Pause at the attorney review gate and explain that the system is not replacing judgment; it is creating a structured review surface.
4. End on the final report: executive summary, risk matrix, prioritized findings, and next-step recommendations.

## Success standard

The workflow is demo-ready when a non-technical legal professional can understand:

- what the workflow accepts,
- what analysis it performs,
- why the output is useful,
- where human review happens,
- and which downstream legal task should happen next.
